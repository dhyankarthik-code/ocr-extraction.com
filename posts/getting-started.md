# Getting Started with Infy Galaxy OCR

Welcome to **Infy Galaxy**, your go-to solution for free, high-accuracy Optical Character Recognition (OCR). In this guide, we’ll show you how to digitize your documents in seconds.

## Why Use Infy Galaxy OCR?

*   **It’s Free:** No hidden costs, ever.
*   **High Accuracy:** Powered by advanced Vision LLM technology.
*   **Secure:** Your files are processed securely and deleted after extraction.

## Step-by-Step Guide

1.  **Upload:** Drag and drop your image (JPG, PNG, PDF, etc.) into the upload box on our home page.
2.  **Process:** Click "Extract Text". Our AI will analyze the document structure.
3.  **Result:** View your extracted text, copy it to your clipboard, or download it as a text file.

## Advanced Features

Did you know you can also verify the confidence score of the extraction? We provide detailed metadata to verify the accuracy of the output.
